About the Author

Utkarsh, the author of "The Emptiness We Share", is a contemporary novelist known for his deeply introspective storytelling. His works often explore the subtle emotional landscapes of human connection and solitude. With a background in literature and philosophy, Utkarsh crafts narratives that linger with readers long after the last page.

When he's not writing, Utkarsh enjoys nature walks, listening to classic jazz, and connecting with readers around the world.
